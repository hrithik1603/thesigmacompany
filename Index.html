Our Story

The Sigma Company was founded with a singular vision: to democratize access to world-class CFO services and financial analytics for businesses of all sizes. We recognized that growing companies needed strategic financial leadership but couldn't always afford full-time C-suite executives.

With over 15 years of combined experience across finance, technology, and consulting, our team has worked with Fortune 500 companies, fast-growing startups, and everything in between. We've seen firsthand how data-driven financial decision-making separates thriving businesses from struggling ones.

Today, we serve 100+ clients across diverse industries, providing fractional CFO services, advanced analytics, and strategic financial consulting. Our mission is to empower business leaders with the insights and expertise they need to make confident decisions and drive sustainable growth.
